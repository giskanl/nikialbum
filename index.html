Niki Zefanya or better known as NIKI is an Indonesian singer who has a career in the United States. NIKI is the most popular artist on Spotify with over 2.5 billion plays. She is also an Indonesian female artist with the record for the highest number of monthly listeners on the platform, reaching 11 million listeners in February 2022. She grew up listening to 1990s R&B music such as Destiny's Child and Aaliyah because of her mother's influence. During her school years, Niki became popular with her covers and original songs on YouTube, reaching more than 40,000 subscribers. NIKI then released the song in her interview. NIKI admitted that she was invited by Rich Brian to join the 88rising label, after Brian heard the song on YouTube and then contacted him directly. In her interview, NIKI said I wanted to make it like a reflection of who I am, like my path, as I grew up with R&B music. I wanted to make sure that element was on the album and I feel like I did that in the chords, the melodies, the structure of the music, I wanted it to be more like an R&B genre. I also wanted there to be a pop element, so people would like it.
